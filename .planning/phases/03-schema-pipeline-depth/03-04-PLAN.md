| comparison findings -> compatibility claims | Test summaries and assertions must not overstate full OpenCC, compiled payload, or correction/tolerance parity. |

| T-03-04-02 | Tampering | `schema_install.rs` filter/translator config | mitigate | Preserve resource-ID validation and do not construct filesystem paths from raw schema strings for OpenCC or dictionary data. |
| T-03-04-03 | Spoofing | OpenCC compatibility claims | mitigate | Tests and summary must state limited built-in map/filter-chain behavior and must not claim full OpenCC conversion-data parity per D-13. |
| T-03-04-04 | Repudiation | correction/tolerance findings | mitigate | Compiled-payload-dependent gaps must be recorded with target phase `04-compiled-dictionary-data` and observed/expected behavior fields. |
</threat_model>
